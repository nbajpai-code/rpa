## ü§ñ Robotic Process Automation (RPA) & AI
*   **Tungsten Automation**: Covers the use of Robotic Process Automation (RPA) combined with Artificial Intelligence (AI) for intelligent automation.
*   **Types of RPA**:
    *   **Ema AI** outlines the three main types of RPA:
        1.  **Attended**: Works alongside humans.
        2.  **Unattended**: Runs autonomously in the background.
        3.  **Hybrid**: A combination of both.
