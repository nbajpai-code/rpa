# Robotics, Automation & RPA YouTube Channels

A curated list of YouTube channels for learning robotics, diverse automation technologies, and Robotic Process Automation (RPA).

## ü§ñ Robotics Learning & Projects

### **Programming & DIY**
*   **[DroneBot Workshop](https://www.youtube.com/c/Dronebotworkshop)**: Excellent deep-dives into motors, sensors, and microcontrollers (Arduino/ESP32) for robotics.
*   **[James Bruton](https://www.youtube.com/user/jamesbruton)**: High-level engineering and 3D printed robotics projects (e.g., OpenDog, bipedal robots).
*   **[How To Mechatronics](https://www.youtube.com/user/DejanNedelkovski)**: comprehensive tutorials on Arduino, sensors, and mechatronic systems.
*   **[Robojax](https://www.youtube.com/user/robojaxTV)**: Extensive tutorials on coding for Arduino and hardware integration.

### **Advanced Robotics & ROS**
*   **[The Construct](https://www.youtube.com/c/TheConstruct)**: The definitive source for ROS (Robot Operating System) tutorials, simulations, and tips.
*   **[Boston Dynamics](https://www.youtube.com/user/BostonDynamics)**: Showcases state-of-the-art robots like Spot and Atlas.
*   **[MIT Robotics](https://www.youtube.com/user/MITRobotics)**: Advanced research and demos from MIT's labs.
*   **[IEEE Robotics and Automation Society](https://www.youtube.com/user/IEEEras)**: Talks, conference presentations, and research highlights.

### **Industrial Robotics**
*   **[Universal Robots](https://www.youtube.com/user/UniversalRobotsVideo)**: Official channel for cobots (collaborative robots).
*   **[ABB Robotics](https://www.youtube.com/user/abbr)**: Industrial automation solutions and case studies.
*   **[KUKA Robot Group](https://www.youtube.com/user/KukaRobotGroup)**: Industrial robot demos and applications.
